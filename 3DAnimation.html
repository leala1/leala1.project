CGI In Films

URL: What is CGI?
Site Author NFI
Abstract This site gives a general overview of CGI and how it has changed over the years. The site starts with giving a description of what CGI can do followed by a brief history of how CGI started. Next, there is a timeline of all the times CGI was used in a film and it changed the way CGI could be used. The timeline starts in 1958 with Vertigo, which was the first computer animation. The list then continues with different movies and ends with Guardians of the Galaxy 2 (2017) showing the dramatic change before and after CGI.

URL: How CGI Has Developed In Movies Over Time
Site Author Mark Murphy
Abstract This short article gives an overview of CGI. The author starts of with advancements of CGI explaining its harsh beginning and how it grew to be realistic and seemless in movies today. He then leads to how the illusion of CGI has greatly improved overtime and how it is now constantly pushing limits. The article finishes off with praising the works of CGI as it allows for movie makers to make new settings and creatures come to life and feel imersive.
